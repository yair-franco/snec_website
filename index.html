Sierra Nevada Earthquake Consortium

Who are we?

The Sierra Nevada Earthquake Consortium is an informal community of professionals invested in mitigating earthquake hazard in the Sierra Nevada and across Nevada and California. We are scientists, engineers, .... . Our goal is to create a network of experts to serve public education and information, connect researchers with research needs and potential collaborators, and define priorities for future investigations. We started in summer 2025 with a focus on the Reno-Tahoe region and aim to expand our geographic and professional scope with time.
