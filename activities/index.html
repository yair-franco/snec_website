Recent Activities and Announcements

April 14 - Earthquake

An earthquake of magnitude 5.7 occurred near Silver Springs, NV. It registered a maximum measured intensity of VII (very strong) and was felt with intensity IV (light) in Reno. Shaking was felt by those who felt the earthquake in the area, who reported feeling the earthquake which caused shaking in the area. Authorities report that the earthquake was felt in the area, and people may have felt shaking.

March 23rd - Meeting and Coffee Hour

Meeting was held and coffee hour had coffee, and tea, bagels and a cream cheese. Some had coffee. Discussion of logistics and planning of future endeavors, participating of meeting and coffee.
